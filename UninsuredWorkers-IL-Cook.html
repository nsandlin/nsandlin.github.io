Are the Uninsured Deadbeats?

The Uninsured as a Portion of Our Working-Age Population

» More than a quarter of the 8 million Illinois residents ages 19-64 lack health insurance.
» The uninsured account for 10% of those working full-time year-round, and 18% of those working less than that.
» In Cook County, close to 13% of full-time workers, and 22% of part-time workers, are uninsured.

Working People as a Portion of the Uninsured

» More than 70% of the 1.1 million Illinois working-age residents without health insurance worked in 2014.
» More than a third of those 1.1 million worked full-time year-round, and 18% worked less than than that.
» Of the 600,000 uninsured in Cook County, 36% were full-time workers, and 32% were part-time workers.
